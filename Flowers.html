Floriography: Language of the Flowers

Introduction

The language of the flowers is an element of history that I find very interesting. As far back as the Victorian era, floriography has been used as coded communication
through flower species. The color of each flower may also alter the coded message. For example, a red rose represents love, whereas a yellow rose represents
friendship. The symbolism of flowers has been utilized in literature such as by Shakespeare, Jane Austen, and other famous authors.

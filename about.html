About Me!

My name is Solymar Kneale which comes from the Spanish words sun and sea (Sol y Mar). I come from a very artistic family and very much enjoy digital art, watercolor, and pottery. I'm a born and raised Coloradan and spent my childhood living in the mountains. Currently I am pursuing a major in CTD (Creative Technology and Design) with a minor in Computer Science at the University of Colorado Boulder. I hope my future leads to a job where I can be creative while making people smile in the process.
